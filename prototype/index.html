One click installs CLS++ to this Mac from this page — no zip, no Terminal, no extra scripts.

How you use CLS++

  • Chat — Use ChatGPT, Claude, or Gemini in the browser. CLS++ adds your saved context in the background; you keep chatting normally.
  • Menu bar — After install, 🧠 appears at the top of the screen. Click it for CLS++ options. macOS may ask for Accessibility once — choose Allow so it can see chat text.
  • Memories — Use Memories (top right on this page) to see what was remembered.
  • Chrome / Brave / Arc — Optional: load the browser extension in one click from the local page.
